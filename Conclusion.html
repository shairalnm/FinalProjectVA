Summary

Answers that we were able to extract from our visualizations were:

  1. The highest number of visitors are from California, followed by Texas and then Florida.
  2. Highest number of forms submitted are for Product Refinance followed by Product Personal.
  3. The least popular products reported are Credit Analyzer and Debt Relief.
  4. Only 2% of Form started are actually getting submitted. So, there is approx. 97% drop from Form Start to Form Submit.
  5. The Major drop is from Form Start to Form Engagement 1 which is approx. 82%.
  6. The topmost source of visit to lending tree is Google.
  7. Only 64% of visitor are actually starting the form.
