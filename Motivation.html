Motivation

The motivation for this project is to know or understand what the visitors do on the website. Specifically, to visualise what pages people visit and what actions they take while visiting. With this data you can determine which pages on your website are most likely to convert visitors into leads or sales. And we would liketo answer the following questions:

  • How far do the visitors reach in a form, Where is the major drop in the form interaction?
  • Which state has more site visitors?
  • What products do the visitor use the most?
  • Do the visitor complete the form?
  • Which source is used more to access the site?
